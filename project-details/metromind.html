The Challenge

Urban commuters often rely on multiple public transportation modes, but information about routes, schedules, and transfers is scattered across different platforms. Users face challenges such as:

  • Difficulty planning multi-modal trips
  • Unclear or inconsistent transport information
  • Limited visibility of public transport options in one place

These issues make daily commuting less efficient and more frustrating.

The Solution

MetroMind was designed as an integrated urban mobility app that brings public transportation information into a single, cohesive experience. Key solutions include:

  • Centralized access to routes, schedules, and transport modes
  • Clear navigation to support multi-modal journey planning
  • A user-focused interface that simplifies complex transit systems

By unifying transport data and prioritizing usability, MetroMind helps users move through the city more efficiently and confidently.
