The Challenge

Many people want to track their finances, but most financial apps feel too complex, rigid, or overwhelming for everyday use. Users often struggle with:

  • Interfaces filled with too much information
  • Financial terms that feel intimidating
  • Limited accessibility for first-time or casual users

As a result, monitoring daily spending becomes more stressful than helpful.

The Solution

EcoFit was designed as a simple and accessible financial tracking app that focuses on clarity and usability. Key design solutions include:

  • A clean dashboard that highlights essential financial information
  • Intuitive navigation for quick access to spending insights
  • A user-friendly layout that supports healthy financial habits

By prioritizing simplicity and accessibility, EcoFit helps users stay aware of their finances without feeling overwhelmed.
