The Challenge

Student housing residents often rely on multiple channels to access information, services, and announcements within their living environment. Common challenges include:

  • Scattered information across chat groups, notice boards, and websites
  • Difficulty accessing housing services quickly
  • Limited engagement between residents and housing management

This fragmentation makes daily campus living less efficient and harder to manage.

The Solution

BINUS Square Mobile was designed as a centralized campus living app that connects residents with essential housing information and services in one streamlined experience. Key solutions include:

  • Central access to announcements, services, and housing information
  • Simple navigation tailored to student needs
  • A clean interface that supports communication and engagement

By unifying campus living features into one platform, the app helps students manage daily activities more efficiently and stay connected to their community.
